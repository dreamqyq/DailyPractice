实现一个 EatMan

说明:实现一个 EatMan，EatMan 可以有以下一些行为

  1. EatMan('Hank'); 输出

    • Hi This is Hank!

  2. EatMan('Hank').eat('dinner').eat('supper'); 输出

    • Hi This is Hank!
    • Eat dinner~
    • Eat supper~

  3. EatMan('Hank').eat('dinner').eatFirst('lunch'); 输出

    • Eat lunch~
    • Hi This is Hank!
    • Eat dinner~

  4. EatMan('Hank').eat('dinner').eatFirst('lunch').eatFirst('breakfast'); 输出

    • Eat breakfast~
    • Eat lunch~
    • Hi This is Hank!
    • Eat dinner~
